## 👋 Welcome

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **OGX Studio**.

We are a technology research lab, product company, and software engineering studio building the future through responsible innovation.

Our mission is simple:

> **Advance technology that benefits humanity.**

We believe technology—especially Artificial Intelligence—has the power to solve meaningful problems, improve lives, and unlock opportunities never before possible. But innovation must be accompanied by responsibility, transparency, and safety.

Every project we build is guided by one principle:

> **Technology should empower people—not replace them.**
